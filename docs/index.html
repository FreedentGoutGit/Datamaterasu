The impact of climate change

By 2050, climate change will cause 14.5 million deaths and $12.5 trillion in losses. Healthcare costs will rise by $1.1 trillion.

Floods will lead to 8.5 million deaths. Droughts will cause 3.2 million deaths. Heat waves will cost $7.1 trillion in lost productivity.

Mosquito-borne diseases will spread, putting 500 million more people at risk.

Source: World Economic Forum : Insight report January 2024
